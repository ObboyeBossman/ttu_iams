Attendance Guidelines
How does GPS verification work?
Your device's GPS is captured at check-in and compared against your registered placement coordinates. Distances over 500m are automatically flagged for review.
What if GPS is unavailable?
Enable location permissions in your browser. If GPS repeatedly fails after several attempts, the system will allow check-in but mark the location source as unverified.
Can I log an absence in advance?
Absences can only be filed for the current calendar day. Contact your liaison officer for retroactive records.
