Liaison Registration Guidelines
Why is GPS location recorded?
Our audit system utilizes device-recorded geolocation to confirm physical presence during company placement setups. This helps supervisors schedule verification visits efficiently.
Can I register before arriving at the company?
No. We strongly advise that you complete Step 2 only once you are fully situated inside the organization premises to guarantee coordinate alignment.
What if my browser blocks GPS permissions?
If the system fails to grab coordinates after 4 automatic attempts, it will dynamically enable a manual override option so you don't get stuck.
